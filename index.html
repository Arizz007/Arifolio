About me

I am a self-trained and highly enthusiastic 3D artist who specializes in photorealistic rendering and environment design, as well as Physically Based Rendering (PBR) workflows for creating game assets. My career has also enabled me to become a skilled 3D visualizer, who enjoys creating product animations with captivating CGI backgrounds and color compositions.

Currently, I work at Wow Labz as a 3D Generalist, contributing to the creation of immersive and visually captivating content. My diverse skill set, developed through self-directed education, enables me to provide cutting-edge solutions and ensure a high level of quality in all of my projects. My expertise spans across a broad range of software tools, such as Blender; 3ds Max; Unreal Engine 5; Unity; various Substance software; Premiere Pro; Photoshop; ZBrush; and more.
